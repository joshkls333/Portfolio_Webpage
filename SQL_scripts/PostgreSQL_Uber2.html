Select the client_uuid, firstname, lastname, email address and phone number of unfulfilled requests on uberX (vehicle_view_id = 1535), in Manila (city_id = 200) during year 2015 (use column request_timestamp_local).

 

SELECT

  t.client_uuid,

  c.firstname,

  c.lastname,

  c.email,

  c.mobile

FROM dim_client as c

JOIN fact_trip as t

  ON c.user_uuid = t.client_uuid

WHERE t.city_id = 200

AND t.vehicle_view_id = 1535

AND t.request_timestamp_local BETWEEN '2015-01-01' AND '2016-01-01'

AND t.status = 'unfulfilled'

 

Find city_name and request_timestamp_utc in two different timezones: 'Europe/Amsterdam' and 'America/Chicago' (show as timestamp_amsterdam and timestamp_chicago) for all trips done in United States (country_id = 1) or the Netherlands (country_id = 125).

 

SELECT

  city_name,

  request_timestamp_utc AT TIME ZONE 'Europe/Amsterdam' as timestamp_amsterdam,

  request_timestamp_utc AT TIME ZONE 'America/Chicago' as timestamp_chicago

FROM dim_city c

JOIN fact_trip t

  ON t.city_id = c.city_id

WHERE c.country_id = 1

   OR c.country_id = 125

 

 

 

Count the number of trips (show as trip_count) which began no earlier than 1 year ago UTC time. The trips must use fare split and the original fare in USD must be greater than 10.

SELECT count(uuid) as trip_count

FROM fact_trip

WHERE begintrip_timestamp_local > now() - interval  '1 year'

  AND is_split_fare IS TRUE

  AND original_fare_usd > 10

Show how many months have passed since the first trip of all clients who completed more than 100 trips. Also show the first and last name of the respective people.

 

SELECT

  firstname,

  lastname,

  datediff('month', first_trip_request_timestamp, now())

FROM dim_client d

JOIN analytics_client a

  ON a.user_uuid = d.user_uuid

WHERE lifetime_trips > 100

 

For drivers who signed up in Manila (id = 200) in the last month, how many days on average does it take to be activated from signup? Show the average as DaysPassed.

 

SELECT avg(datediff('day', signup_timestamp, first_active_timestamp  )) as DaysPassed

FROM DIM_DRIVER

WHERE signup_city_id = 200

AND signup_timestamp > now() - interval '1 month'

 

For clients who signed up in the last year in region India SC - East & Central, how many days on average it take between the signup and their first trip? Show the average as 'activation_days'.

 

SELECT

  avg(datediff('day', signup_timestamp, first_trip_request_timestamp)) as activation_days

FROM analytics_client a

JOIN dim_city c

  ON c.city_id = a.signup_city_id

WHERE region = 'India SC - East & Central'

AND signup_timestamp > now() - interval '1 year'

 

Show each city name with its launch_date truncated to full quarters. Show only those cities which are operational and are in the US.

 

SELECT

  city_name,

  date_trunc('quarter', launch_date)

FROM dim_city

WHERE is_operational IS TRUE

  AND country_name = 'United States'

 

Show the timestamp of first requested trip for each client together with their first and last name for those users who signed up with the 'Organic' channel. Truncate the timestamp to days.

An easy one to start off. Select the trip distance and trip duration for every trip which began between November 14, 2015 2 PM and December 21, 2015 5 AM UTC time. The trips must be completed.

 

SELECT

  trip_distance_miles,

  trip_duration_seconds

FROM fact_trip

WHERE begintrip_timestamp_utc BETWEEN '2015-11-14 14:00' AND '2015-12-21 05:00'

AND is_completed IS TRUE

 

Show each driver's firstname, lastname and the number of active vehicles for each driver who completed their first trip in December 2015 and whose lifetime rating is higher than 3. Use a left join.

 

SELECT

  firstname,

  lastname,

  num_active_vehicles

FROM analytics_driver a

LEFT JOIN dim_driver d

  ON d.driver_uuid = a.driver_uuid

WHERE first_completed_trip_timestamp BETWEEN '2015-12-01' AND '2015-12-01'::date + interval '1 month'

  AND lifetime_rating > 3
